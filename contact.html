Plan Your Visit

What to Expect When You Visit

1

Arrival

Arrive 15-20 minutes early to find parking and get settled. Our parking attendants will guide you to available spaces.

2

Welcome

Our friendly ushers will greet you at the entrance, provide you with a bulletin, and help you find a seat.

3

Kids Check-In

If you have children, visit our Kids Church check-in desk. Our secure check-in system ensures your child's safety.

4

Enjoy the Service

Relax and enjoy the worship, teaching, and fellowship. Services typically last 1.5-2 hours.

5

Connect

After service, visit our connect desk to learn more about the church, meet our pastors, and get involved.

Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to church?

Come as you are! While some dress formally, others prefer casual attire. The most important thing is that you're comfortable and ready to worship.

Is there parking available?

Yes! We have ample parking space around the church premises. Our parking attendants will guide you to available spots.

Do you have programs for children?

Absolutely! We have a vibrant Kids Church program for ages 3-12 during our 10:30 AM Sunday service, with age-appropriate lessons and activities.

How long are your services?

Our services typically last between 1.5 to 2 hours, including worship, teaching, and ministry time.

Can I watch services online?

Yes! We livestream our Sunday services on Facebook and YouTube. You can also access past sermons on our YouTube channel.

How can I become a member?

We'd love to have you! Contact us to learn about our membership process, which includes attending our Welcome Class and meeting with our pastoral team.

Still Have Questions?

Don't hesitate to reach out. We're always happy to help and answer any questions you may have about our church, services, or ministries.
