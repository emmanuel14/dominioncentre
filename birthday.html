Celebrating A Man of God

Honoring Pastor Perez Araka

The countdown continues as we prepare to honor Pastor Perez Araka - a father in the faith, an exceptional leader, a visionary, and a mentor who has shaped countless destinies with wisdom, excellence, and Kingdom passion.

This special celebration is more than just a birthday; it's a moment to recognize the profound impact Pastor Perez has made in the lives of individuals, families, and communities through his unwavering dedication to God's work.
