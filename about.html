Our Story

The Birth of RCCG Dominion Centre

The Redeemed Christian Church of God, Dominion Centre (RCCGDC) was birthed on the 9th of October, 2022. Born out of a divine mandate and vision to reach more souls for Christ, our church represents a new chapter in the RCCG journey.

We are a Satellite Church of REDEEMED CHRISTIAN CHURCH OF GOD, THE KING'S PALACE PARISH HEADQUARTERS OF REGION 33. This connection roots us in a rich heritage while positioning us to embrace innovative approaches to ministry.

RCCGDC is an expression church which brands the future look of RCCG. We are committed to maintaining the core values and doctrines of the Redeemed Christian Church of God while embracing contemporary methods of worship, teaching, and community engagement.

Our Mission

To practically apply scriptural principles of dominion in every sphere of life — empowering believers to live victoriously and finish well in their divine assignments.

  • Spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ
  • Nurture spiritual growth and maturity
  • Build a strong community of believers
  • Impact society through service

Our Vision

To raise Kingdom stars of today and the future who will rule, reign, and have dominion over the seven mountains of influence — Religion, Family, Business (Finance & Economy), Government (Power), Education, Arts & Entertainment (including Sports), and Media.

  • Raise leaders for the kingdom
  • Establish a culture of excellence
  • Pioneer innovative ministry approaches
  • Expand God's kingdom globally

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Word-Centered

We believe the Bible is God's inspired, infallible Word and the foundation for faith and practice.

Spirit-Led

We depend on the Holy Spirit for guidance, power, and transformation in all aspects of ministry.

Love & Unity

We cultivate a community of authentic love, acceptance, and genuine fellowship among believers.

Excellence

We pursue excellence in all we do, honoring God with our best efforts and highest standards.

Service

We are committed to serving God, each other, and our community with humility and joy.

Growth

We foster continuous spiritual growth and personal development for every member.

What We Believe

Our core Values

Love and Fellowship

Holiness and Purity

Excellence and Intentionality
