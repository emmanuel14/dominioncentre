Our Story

The Birth of RCCG Dominion Centre

The Redeemed Christian Church of God, Dominion Centre (RCCGDC) was birthed on the 9th of October, 2022. Born out of a divine mandate and vision to reach more souls for Christ, our church represents a new chapter in the RCCG journey.

We are a Satellite Church of REDEEMED CHRISTIAN CHURCH OF GOD, THE KING'S PALACE PARISH HEADQUARTERS OF REGION 33.

An Expression Church which brands the future look of the RCCG. We are a church for the younger generation (The Future), for professionals, entrepreneurs and career-minded worshippers, committed to impacting the immediate community for Jesus. Our foundation is rooted in the original ideals of the main church - The Word, Prayer, Holiness, Soul Winning and manifestation of the gifts of the Holy Spirit.

Lead Pastor, RCCG Dominion Centre

Dr. Perez Araka is a passionate teacher of God's Word, a visionary leader, and a devoted servant of Christ. With a heart for raising overcomers and establishing kingdom dominion, he leads RCCG Dominion Centre with wisdom, grace, and prophetic insight.

Known for his practical, life-changing messages and his commitment to developing leaders, Pastor Perez creates an atmosphere where believers can experience genuine transformation and discover their divine purpose.

Co-Pastor & Women's Leader

Dcns. Greamba Perez Araka (GPA)

Wife of the Lead Pastor & Co-founder, Jesus Cares Family Outreach

Greamba Perez Araka (GPA), is the wife of the Lead Pastor of RCCG DC - Dr. Perez Araka. She's also the Co-founder of Jesus Cares Family Outreach and Dream-Aid Community Charity Trust; NGOs aimed at uplifting the less privileged in society, feeding and caring for the needs of children and ensuring that youths channel their energy to learning a skill.

GPA, as she's fondly called, has a burning desire for the education of children in society especially education for the girl-child which has largely been de-emphasized in certain parts of the country. She founded the Sisters Fellowship of RCCG Dominion Centre, where she uses it as a platform to bring sisters together to learn, bond, aspire to be better ladies and ultimately uphold Christian virtues as a lady.

By the grace of God, she influences people by being real as much as possible, living her life by example. Her lifestyle, character, attitude, disposition and opinion in sensitive issues speaks for itself. GPA has a special love for children and teenagers and as such, she commits as much as she can to their all-round well-being.

What We Believe

Statement of Faith

01

The Holy Scripture

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God, inerrant in its original writings, and the supreme authority in all matters of faith and conduct.

02

The Godhead

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, co-equal in nature, power, and glory.

03

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God, born of a virgin, lived a sinless life, died for our sins, rose from the dead, and ascended to heaven.

04

Salvation

We believe salvation is a free gift of God's grace, received through faith in Jesus Christ, resulting in spiritual regeneration and eternal life.

05

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit, speaking in tongues as the Spirit gives utterance, and the manifestation of spiritual gifts.

06

The Church

We believe the Church is the Body of Christ, composed of all believers, called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, ministry, and evangelism.

07

Second Coming

We believe in the personal, visible return of Jesus Christ to earth and the resurrection of both the saved and the lost.

08

Water Baptism

We believe in water baptism by immersion as an outward expression of an inward faith and identification with Christ's death and resurrection.
