Who We Are

Welcome Home

The Redeemed Christian Church of God, Dominion Centre (RCCGDC) was birthed on the 9th of October, 2022. It is a Satellite Church of REDEEMED CHRISTIAN CHURCH OF GOD, THE KING'S PALACE PARISH HEADQUARTERS OF REGION 33.

We are an expression church which brands the future look of RCCG, committed to spiritual growth, fellowship, and transforming lives through the power of God's Word.
