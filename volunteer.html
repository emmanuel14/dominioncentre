Why Volunteer?

Looking to serve and grow in your faith? We're excited to welcome you into one of our church departments! Whether you love music, teaching, helping others, or working behind the scenes, there's a place for you here.

Volunteering at RCCG Dominion Centre is more than just servingâ€”it's about discovering your purpose, developing your gifts, and making a lasting impact in the lives of others. When you serve, you become part of a community that is transforming Port Harcourt and beyond.
