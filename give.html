Common Questions

Giving FAQs

Why do we give?

Giving is an act of worship and gratitude. We give to honor God, support His work, and help those in need. It’s our way of showing thankfulness for His blessings.

Can I designate my giving to a specific cause?

Yes. You can specify whether your donation is for Tithe, Offering, Building Project, Outreach, or Welfare, and it will be directed accordingly.

What's the difference between tithes and offerings?

A tithe is 10% of your income given to God as an act of obedience and worship (Malachi 3:10). Offerings are gifts given above and beyond your tithe, as the Holy Spirit leads.

Can I give even if I’m not a member of the church?

Absolutely! Anyone who feels led to support God’s work through our ministry is welcome to give.

What if I can’t give financially right now?

Giving isn’t only about money — your time, service, and prayers are also powerful gifts to God’s Kingdom.
